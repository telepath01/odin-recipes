Stuffed Manicotti

The stuffed manicotti is an all time favorite of mine. This dish brings the best of both worlds (pasta and cheese). The stuffing is prepared first, by mixing the cheeses together with the eggs. When the stuffing is complete you then pull out the baking pan and add marinara sauce to the bottom, this will be your first layer. Then you put the manicotti to boil, when the pasta is ready, you start stuffing each one and placing them one at a time on the sauce. When you have completed a layer of manicotti you then spread marinara sauce over the top and you finish by spreading your chesses evenly over the sauce. When the oven is ready you place your dish in for 45 minutes at a slow bake of 350 degrees. When the meal is cooked you can then cut into individual sections and enjoy.

    Ingrediants

  • Manicotti Shells
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Eggs

    Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a bowl combine one cup mozzarella cheese, parmesan, ricotta and the eggs.
  3. Boil the Manicotti till the shells are soft.
  4. Pour marinara sauce in a baking pan.
  5. Stuff the manicotti one at a time and place them on the suace.
  6. When you have filled the pan with a layer of stuffed manicotti, pour the remaining marinara sauce on top of the manicotti.
  7. Spread the mozzarella and parmesan chesse evenly on the top of the sauce.
  8. Place the pan in the oven.
  9. Upon completion cut into portions and enjoy.
