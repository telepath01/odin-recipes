Puertorican Style Turkey

The Famous Puertorican Turkey

This mouth-watering turkey will leave you wanting more. This meal is prepared the day prior by creating a paste from the ingredients. When the paste is ready you must lather the past on the turkey in all its spots, you must also cut slits in order to stuff the turkey with the paste.

On the day of cooking, you place the turkey in an oven bag and slowly bake it for the correct amount of time. When the turkey is done baking you can carve it to your desired amount and watch as the meat falls off the bone.

    Ingrediants

  • Turkey
  • Turkey Roasting Pan
  • Turkey Bag
  • Adobo
  • Sazzon
  • Butter
  • Garlic

    Steps

    Day 1
  1. Thaw Turkey
  2. Place thawed turkey on a metal sheet
  3. Groung 4 Garlic Bulbs into a paste
  4. Mix your Adobo to the garlic paste
  5. Mix 4 packs of Sazzon into your paste
  6. Cut 2 inch holes into various locations of the turkey
  7. Stuff holes with past
  8. Cut butter into squares
  9. Stuff butter into stuffed holes
  10. Use remaining paste to cover the complete turkey inside and out
  11. Place Turkey into Oven Bag
    12. Day 2
  12. Prepare oven to Bake at 350 degrees
  13. Cut a small hole in turkey bag
  14. Place Turkey in oven (Remember 13 minutes for evrey pound)
  15. Upon completion carve and enjoy
